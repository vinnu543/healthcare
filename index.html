Our Hospital Values, Vison, Mission

Our Mission

The mission is to provide high quality, patient centered, primary health care for all through a successful, reproducible and economically sustainable model.

Our Vision

We will be the provider of choice for those seeking high quality health care and the employer of choice for those engaged in the care of others.

Our Value

We believe that
  • Access to high quality health care is a fundamental human right.
  • High-quality primary health care has to be patient-centered, culturally competent and holistic
  • Health care should be rendered regardless of socioeconomic status, race, nationality, sex, age,background, belief, or circumstance.
  • The well being of our Staff and volunteers is as important as that of the patients we serve
